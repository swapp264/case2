Success Story: Luna

From Street to Home

Luna was found injured and malnourished on the streets. Through our comprehensive care program, she received medical treatment, rehabilitation, and found her forever home with the Sharma family.

Timeline:

  • Day 1: Emergency rescue and initial medical assessment
  • Week 1-2: Intensive medical care and nutrition program
  • Month 1-2: Socialization and behavioral training
  • Month 3: Successful adoption to loving family

Luna's story represents our commitment to every animal's complete journey from rescue to rehabilitation to rehoming.

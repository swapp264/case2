Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common pet care questions

üêï Feeding & Nutrition

What should I feed a stray dog? +

For stray dogs, provide clean water and high-quality dog food. Avoid human food like chocolate, onions, or grapes. If you can't get dog food immediately, boiled rice with boiled chicken (no spices) is a safe temporary option.

How often should I feed a stray animal? +

Feed adult dogs 1-2 times daily, puppies 3-4 times. Cats prefer small, frequent meals. Always provide fresh water. Adjust portions based on the animal's size and condition.

üè• Health & First Aid

How to treat minor wounds on pets? +

Clean the wound with mild soap and water. Apply antiseptic solution (diluted betadine). Cover with clean bandage if possible. Monitor for signs of infection (redness, swelling, discharge). Seek veterinary care for deep wounds.

What are signs of a sick animal? +

Look for: lethargy, loss of appetite, vomiting, diarrhea, coughing, sneezing, limping, unusual behavior, discharge from eyes/nose, changes in drinking/urination. Contact a vet if symptoms persist.

üè† Shelter & Care

How to provide temporary shelter? +

Use cardboard boxes, plastic containers, or build simple shelters with wood and tarpaulin. Ensure they're waterproof, elevated from ground, and insulated. Provide clean bedding (old blankets, straw).

How to approach a scared animal? +

Move slowly, avoid direct eye contact, speak softly. Offer food from a distance. Don't chase or corner them. Let them come to you. Use treats to build trust gradually.
